How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Fractalito account using either option below.

  • In the app: Profile → Settings → Danger Zone → Delete account
  • Email us at altay.said@dataklub.xyz with subject "Delete my account"

How to Delete Only Your Moments

You can delete only your moments (keep the account) using either option below.

  • In the app: Profile → Settings → Danger Zone → Delete all moments
  • Email us at altay.said@dataklub.xyz with subject "Delete my moments"

What Gets Deleted

Your account and all moments/timeline data stored in the app.

Retention Period

Deleted immediately.
