Fractalito Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026
Contact: privacy@fractalito.com

1. Introduction

Fractalito ("we", "our", or "the app") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. Our goal is simple: help you capture your moments while keeping your information safe.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User-Created Content

  • Notes, text, tags, and attachments (photos, links) you create in the app.
  • Temporal and spatial metadata (timestamps, X/Y positions on your timeline).
  • Purpose: To store and display your moments within the app.

2.2 Account Data (Optional)

  • Email and user ID (if you choose to create an account).
  • Purpose: To identify your account and allow you to log in.

2.3 Device & App State Data

  • App version, operating system type, and timezone.
  • Purpose: Improve app functionality and ensure compatibility.

2.4 Analytics & Diagnostics (Optional)

  • Aggregated usage data to improve features.
  • Crash reports including stack traces, device info, and app version.
  • Note: No user content is included in analytics or crash reports.

2.5 Storing Information About Family Members

Users may choose to record moments for family members, including children. Fractalito is designed for adults to capture personal memories.

  • Important: Avoid entering personally identifiable information about minors (full name, date of birth, location, school, or other sensitive details) unless you are the parent or guardian and consent to storing it.
  • Privacy by default: All moments are private and stored securely; only you can access them unless you explicitly share.
  • We do not market the app to children, and the app is not intended for children under 13.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • Contacts
  • Location (unless user attaches media with geotags)
  • Microphone or camera access except user-triggered attachments
  • Clipboard data
  • Advertising or tracking identifiers

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To display and organize your moments in the timeline.
  • To provide account login functionality (if enabled).
  • To fix app crashes and improve features.
  • To understand feature usage through aggregated analytics (anonymized).

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • User-created moments, including those related to family members, remain stored until you delete them.
  • You can delete any moment or your account at any time via the app or by contacting support.
  • Crash and diagnostic data are anonymized and retained only to improve app stability.

6. Permissions

  • The app requests permissions only when needed.
  • Example: Photo library access is requested only if you attach a photo to a moment.
  • You can revoke permissions at any time from your device settings.

7. Security

  • All user data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections.
  • Access to your data is limited to authorized systems and personnel.

8. Children’s Privacy

  • Fractalito is not intended for children under 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect data directly from children.
  • Any data about minors is provided by adult users (parents/guardians) and should be entered responsibly.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this policy occasionally.
  • Significant changes will be communicated within the app and the effective date updated.

10. Contact Us
